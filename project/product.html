Ranunculus Bulbs

Ranunculus (Random Color) bulbs are popular flowering plants known for their vibrant and layered petals that come in a mix of colors such as red, pink, yellow, white, and orange.

Rajnigandha

Rajnigandha, also known as Tuberose, is a fragrant flowering plant grown from bulbs. It produces tall spikes of white, waxy flowers that bloom mainly in summer and early autumn.

Tulip

Tulips are popular spring-blooming flowers grown from bulbs,cup-shaped blooms in a wide range of colors. They are usually planted in the autumn and bloom in early to late spring.

Peace Lily, Spathiphyllum

The Peace Lily (Spathiphyllum) is a popular indoor plant known for its elegant white flowers and lush green leaves. It thrives in indirect sunlight and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Jade Plants in Ceramic Pots

Jade plants in ceramic pots are attractive,low-maintenance succulents known for their thick, glossy, green leaves &tree-like appearance. They thrive in bright, indirect sunlight & require minimal watering.

Healthy Home-Office

A healthy home-office is a well-organized, comfortable, and productive workspace that promotes physical and mental well-being.t includes proper lighting, good ventilation, an ergonomic chair and desk setup.

Air Purifying Indoor Plants

Air-purifying indoor plants are perfect for office desks as they enhance air quality and add a touch of greenery to the workspace.Some popular choices include Snake Plant, Peace Lily and Aloe Vera.
